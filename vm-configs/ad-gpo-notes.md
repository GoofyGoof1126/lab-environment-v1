## GPO — Staff Password Policy and Logon Banner

### GPO Details
- Name: Pretorium-Staff-PasswordPolicy
- Linked to: OU=Pretorium-Staff,DC=pretorium,DC=lab
- Status: Enabled

### Password Policy
- Minimum password length: 12 characters
- Password complexity: Enabled

### Logon Banner
- Title: "Pretorium Trust — Authorized Access Only"
- Message: "This system is for authorized use only. All 
  activity is monitored and logged."

### Why this matters
A logon banner isn't just cosmetic — it has real legal weight. 
Without an explicit warning banner, unauthorised access claims 
become harder to prosecute because the user can argue they had 
no notice the system was restricted. This is the same principle 
behind the POPIA training I delivered — policy only has teeth 
if it's communicated and visible, not just written down somewhere.
