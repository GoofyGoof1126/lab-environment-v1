### What This Maps To Legally
POPIA Section 19(1) requires responsible parties to implement 
"appropriate, reasonable technical and organisational measures" 
to prevent loss, damage or unauthorised access to personal 
information. Staff awareness training is an organisational 
measure that directly satisfies this requirement.
